TERMS OF SERVICES

LAST UPDATED JAN 2025

1. Confidentiality

Your information is strictly confidential except in cases of harm to self or others.

2. Appointments

Sessions must be booked at least 2 days in advance. Notify us at least 24 hours in advance for cancellations or rescheduling.

3. Payment

We accept payment only via credit/debit cards or electronic funds transfer at the time of the session.

4. Therapeutic Relationship

We work together with respect and trust towards agreed-upon goals.

5. Boundaries

We maintain professional boundaries and avoid dual relationships.

6. Emergencies

Seek immediate assistance in emergencies; we can provide support and resources.

7. Termination

Either party can end therapy at any time; referrals will be provided if needed.

8. Records

Records are securely maintained; clients can access them upon request.
